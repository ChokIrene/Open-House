Introducing Our App!

NP Open House

NP Open House is an application whereby it makes Open House fun for everybody!
It spices up the event by having a "treasure hunt" around the school. Download this application and you can make this trip a memoriable one for you. Sounds interesting? Download now!

How to play?

- The main task is to go around the school to find QR codes.
- You will be given 20 minutes to find in each schools.
- There will be 2 sets of QR codes, the puzzle and model.
- When you scan the model QR code, a virtual 3D model done by our students will pop up! Do look forward to it!
- Both QR codes will add up to your points. However, the model will worth more points than the
puzzles so do try your best to find them!
- Points will be accumulated after you have been to all the 9 schools.
- You can get rewards depending on the points you get!

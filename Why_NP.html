Why should you choose NP?

Unique programmes:

Ngee Ann provides many unique programmes which provides students with something Xtra. For example, the Personalised Learning Pathway gives opportunity for you to graduate with a Diploma and Minor of your choice. Not only that, the NP Christieara Programme unlocks your talent, hones your leadership qualities and even preps you for Uni scholarship applications. And the Global Entrepreneurial Internship Programme pioneered by NP lets you work in a start-up in one of these bustling global cities: Silicon Valley, Shanghai, Jakarta, etc.

Vibrant and award winning campus:

In NP, we have over 80 student clubs and societies, many cool hangouts and facilities – is where you’d love to be. And be prepared to be wowed by the charms of our beautiful garden campus. Let our natural spaces and innovative ideas inspire you to think and live sustainably!

Our Student Care:

Our Student Care demonstrates our commitment to providing holistic support for our student community. From setting up the NP Student Aid Grant to offering a wide range of scholarships, we ensure no deserving student is denied of a good education. Our one-stop Student Services & Visitors Centre (SSVC), Special Needs Centre and Office of Internships & Careers are purpose-built to serve students’ needs. With the collective efforts of our lecturers, support staff, counsellors, industry mentors and coaches, we seek to provide a caring and conducive environment for you to learn and grow in.

Our Service-Learning Pedagogy:

Our Service-Learning Pedagogy makes us the 1st poly in Singapore to infuse Service-Learning into the curriculum of every diploma. Our success has gained wide recognition and won us the prestigious MacJannet Prize for Global Citizenship Award for exemplary student civic engagement.

Our Illustrious Alumni

Our Illustrious Alumni are our pride and proof that we have raised generations of industry leaders, trailblazers, changemakers, as well as ordinary individuals with Xtraordinary stories. From Secretlab’s Ian Ang, Carousell’s Quek Siu Rui and Marcus Tan, to Carro’s Aaron Tan, our alumni founders are testament of NP’s enterprising spirit. Making their mark on the global stage are award-winning filmmakers from our film school – Anthony Chen, Boo Junfeng and Yeo Siew Hua, just to name a few. Not forgetting our trailblazers Dr Ron Ng and Dr Soong Junwei, who made history for being the first poly grads to get into NUS Medical School.
